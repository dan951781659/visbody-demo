# WellnessHub User Manual
WellnessHub is designed for fitness and wellness organizations, integrating device management, body composition assessment, smart analysis, and training planning. It automatically connects to body scanning devices, generates multi-dimensional health reports in real time with alerts for abnormal metrics, uses AI to create personalized training plans, and provides coaches with member data tracking and trend analysis—supporting an end-to-end workflow: assess, analyze anomalies, create personalized training outlines, and optimize. Data-driven precision and smart tools help you build a more competitive health management service.

#### Health Profile
Click [Health profile] for a user to open their profile. The profile stores basic info, health background, lifestyle, etc.; it is used together with the latest measurement report to generate a more tailored training outline. The page supports [Edit] and [Generate training outline].

**Health profile fields:**

1. Basic info: Name, nickname, gender, age, height, weight, email, phone. Email/phone are the unique identifiers and can only be changed by the user on their side.

2. Health background: Medical history, current medications (optional, max 500 characters), exercise restrictions (optional, e.g. avoid deep squats, no high-intensity cardio, max 500 characters).

3. Lifestyle: Daily activity level and sleep duration (dropdowns).

4. Diet: Meals per day (optional, 0–20), dietary requirements/preferences (dropdown), preferred ingredients (optional, max 500 characters).

5. Goals and preferences: Training goal, frequency, session length, focus areas, injury areas to note, body shape goal, fitness improvement needs (shown based on store AI interpretation settings).

<font style="color:#df2a3f;">Note: Body shape goal and fitness improvement are shown only when the store’s AI interpretation dimension includes the corresponding option.</font>

6. Training outline preference: Training cycle (default 1 month / 4 weeks) and number of phases (default 3).

7. Additional notes: Optional, max 500 characters (experience, conditions, special needs).

#### AI Free Dialogue
From the body report page or anomaly analysis page, click the [Kuafu] button at bottom-right to open AI free dialogue.

**AI suggested questions:** Generated from the current page; you can refresh for other questions. **Quick consult:** Hover over an indicator on the report and click to send it to AI for a quick follow-up. **Free dialogue:** Type any question for an AI reply. **History:** View, search (by title), rename, or delete past conversations. **New conversation:** Start a new topic; new conversations can be auto-created per user for deeper follow-up.

#### AI Report Interpretation
Available when the account is Pro. It generates body agenda analysis and AI training outline from the measurement so you can give better analysis and advice to members.

Status: Not interpreted—click to start; Interpreted—click to open the anomaly analysis page. The system shows up to 5 key body anomalies by priority based on store AI dimensions and the report.

Content includes: Overview (assessment, indicators, core issues, priority map); per-item analysis—Health (up to 5 items: analysis, related factors, key actions, impact, suggestions), Body aesthetics (body type, waist, hips, back, limbs, style guide), Sport (up to 5 items: analysis, related factors, risk chain).

<!-- 这是一张图片，ocr 内容为： -->
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/38897343/1756709607682-512ef859-83bf-469a-9329-87a487588fc2.png)

#### AI Training Outline
Generated from the user’s measurement report, health profile, and store settings. One outline per user per store. When generation is complete, the right panel collapses. Content includes: Plan overview (goals, cycle, principles, plan, expected results, body analysis, risks, contraindications); per-phase details (training: goals, plan, notes; diet: targets, sample day, supplements if a list is uploaded); Summary (terms, success factors, long-term path). You can type modification instructions in the right panel; AI will update the outline. If equipment or supplement lists are uploaded in store settings, they appear in the outline. When the user’s health profile or report is updated, “Update training outline” is shown; click Update to regenerate. You can open from the user list or health profile via [Generate training outline] or [View training outline].

<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">Note: After changing language or metric/imperial units, click [Regenerate] in the top-left to generate the outline in the correct language/units.</font>
